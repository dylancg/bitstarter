Birdlection is a social voting game which rewards thoughtful players. The game is split into two phases: the submission phase and the voting phase. During the submission phase, each player has one minute to submit a brief response to that round's randomly selected question. During the voting phase, the responses are displayed anonymously and each player casts their vote on their favorite answer. The server then counts the votes and reveals the authors and tallies for that round. Whoever has the most votes after ten rounds of questions is the champion -- will you be leader of the flock?
